Company

AIRSYST was created by technicians involved in the overhaul of screw and centrifugal compressors from manufacturers such as Atlas Copco, Ingersoll Rand, Kaeser, Gardner Denver, Compair, BOGE, Dalgakiran and many others. Over the years, our specialists have gained vast experience in the selection of compressor equipment; we provide repair and overhaul service for GHH RAND, ATLAS COPCO, ROTORCOMP, AERZEN, FINI COMPRESSOR, QUINCY airends as well as supply of consumables and spare parts.

The management team pays great attention to the development of other industrial areas such as:

- Supply of nitrogen generators - Supply of oxygen generators - Supply of air separation plants - Supply of heat pumps

The Airsyst group is the official dealer of main and well known premium brands such as : BOGE Kompressoren (Germany); HERTZ (Germany); IDEAL MAKINA (Turkey); FILSAN (Turkey); DENAIR (China); ATS (Italy); SOTRAS (Italy).

In 2022, Airsyst opened its branches in the European Union (Tallinn, Estonia), the Dublin, Ireland and the Russian Federation (Smolensk).

Our goal: To become the largest supplier of industrial equipment spare parts and services in Eurasia, North and South America and Africa.

Our mission: To provide the best service, supply the best equipment in terms of price / quality ratio, affordable and high-quality spare parts and consumables.

Provide an opportunity to supply any spare parts for our customers from the stock, or with a maximum delivery of up to 5 working days.

The team of Airsyst group companies, offers you and your company to evaluate the highest qualification of our specialists for solving any problems in the supply of equipment or spare parts, repair or service.
