About the service center

AIRSYST was created by technicians who involved in the overhaul of screw and centrifugal compressors of well known brands such as Atlas Copco, Ingersoll Rand, Kaeser, Gardner Denver, Compair, BOGE, Dalgakiran and many others. Over the years, our specialists have gained vast experience in the selection of compressed air equipment; oil flooded airend repair services for GHH RAND, ATLAS COPCO, ROTORCOMP, AERZEN, FINI COMPRESSOR, QUINCY, etc.

We have started our business as a small service center for the repair and maintenance of screw compressors from various manufacturers. Gradually, year after year, our engineers have been trained at the manufacturing plants (Hanwha Techwinn (South Korea), Gardner Denver (Finland, Germany), Atlas Copco (Belgium, Turkey), Sullair (Ireland). In last 10 years, our specialists have serviced more than 300 units of oil-lubricated and oil-free compressors and blowers; about 40 overhauls were carried out with the replacement of stages on Atlas Copco oil-free compressors of the ZT/ZR/ZE/ZH series;our service engineers have spent more than 6000 hours while repairing and rebuilding dry type compressors.

Of course, a good repair is impossible to carry out without quality spare parts. Currently, there are 2 options for servicing oil-free compressors:
- service the compressor and air treatment systems at official dealers
Or
- contact our company for re-engineering

If the equipment is still under warranty, it makes sense to work with an official representative. If the compressor warranty is over, then the best option would be to work with a company that offers re-engineering.

Advantages of re-engineering from the AIRSYST group of companies:
- Reducing the costs of the repair and maintenance of Atlas Copco ZT/ZR/ZE/ZH series compressors (savings up to 70% comparing to genuine spare parts)
- We provide all the genuine equivalent spare parts for the Atlas Copco oil free compressors (ZT/ZR/ZE/ZH series).Our non genuine spare and replacement parts are manufactured by (RIEM) Italy or AIRTEC (England) with a 24-month warranty.
- Optimal delivery time for spare parts and components (maximum delivery time up to 30 days)
- A wide range of spare parts for Atlas Copco ZT/ZR/ZE/ZH series compressors from the stock.
- Airsyst can provide a stock management system to its customers.We can stock the service parts in our warehouses on behalf of the customer.
- We provide 24 months warranty for the parts and the labor supplied from us. The warranty can be extended up on(up to 60 months*).
- On site Engineer support maximum 3 days**
- Technical support 24/7***
- Extended service / maintenance contracts.
